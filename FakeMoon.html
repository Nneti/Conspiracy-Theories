The trip to the moon:

Most of the information we know about space exploration is given to us by NASA. It stands for National Aeronautics and Space Administration, NASA is an independent agency of the executive branch of the federal government of the United States. It is responsible for space research, so everything we know comes from them, however, do we know if that information is accurate? Could the government be lying to us in order to protect its image?


What happened to the evidence and data?

Conspiracy theorists claim that N.A.S.A staged the moon landing in order for America to win the space race. Since the mid-1970s, many people believe that NASA and other organizations "knowingly misled the public into believing the landings happened, by manufacturing, tampering with, or destroying evidence including photos, telemetry tapes, radio and TV transmissions, Moon rock samples, and even killing some key witnesses." (Wikipedia)

People have conducted surveys in which they asked others their opinions over the moon landing and whether or not they believe is fake or not. The results was gathered from multiple different places, and the results are shown below!
